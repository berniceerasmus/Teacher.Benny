    How to Play INSTRUCTIONS

    ▸ WORD REVEAL MODE

    1. Clue #1 is shown automatically — it's free.
    2. Click + Reveal Next Clue to unlock Clue #2 or #3. Each extra clue costs points.
    3. Press letters on the on-screen keyboard (or your physical keyboard) to guess the hidden word one letter at a time.
    4. You have 6 wrong guesses before the word is lost — watch the figure!
    5. Solve it with fewer clues and fewer wrong guesses to earn the most points.

    ▸ CROSSWORD MODE

    1. A mini crossword of 5 words is generated automatically.
    2. Click a clue in the list, or click a cell in the grid, to select a word.
    3. Type your answer letter by letter — the cursor moves automatically.
    4. Press Enter or fill the last cell to check your answer.
    5. Use Backspace to correct mistakes.
    6. Unlock extra clues from the Clue File panel on the right — each one costs points.

    Scoring POINTS GUIDE

    Action Points
    Solve with Clue #1 only +50
    Solve with Clue #2 revealed ~+35
    Solve with all 3 clues revealed ~+20
    Each extra clue revealed −5 to −10
    Each wrong letter guess −5
    Minimum score per word 10
    Word Counts per Level:
    🟢 A1 — 10 words  |  🔵 A2 — 20 words
    🟠 B1 — 30 words  |  🔴 B2 — 40 words

    Clue Rules:
    Every word has exactly 3 clues. Clue #1 is always free. Revealing #2 costs −5 pts, revealing #3 costs −10 pts. Use them wisely!

    Stopwatch Timer:
    Toggle the Timer checkbox at the top to see how long you take per word. It counts up — try to beat your own time!
