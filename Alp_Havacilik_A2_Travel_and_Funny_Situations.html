Today’s Goals

Speaking

Talk about travel problems and funny situations.

Grammar

Use the past simple: went, stayed, missed, lost, forgot.

Pronunciation

Practise -ed endings, th, r, v/w.

Confidence

Speak in pairs and small groups before speaking to everyone.

Reading Text

Last month, I travelled to Istanbul for a short weekend trip. First, I woke up late and missed my bus to the station. Then, I took a taxi, but there was a lot of traffic. I arrived at the station five minutes before my train left. I was very nervous.

When I arrived in Istanbul, I could not find my hotel. My phone battery died, and I had no signal. I asked a man for help, and he was very kind. He showed me the way to the hotel. Finally, I checked in, but my room was noisy because it was next to the lift. It was a difficult day, but I learned to prepare better before travelling.
