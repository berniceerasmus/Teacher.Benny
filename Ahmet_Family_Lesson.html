My name is Ali. I am ten years old. I am from Turkey.

I have a big family. My father's name is Mehmet. My mother's name is Fatma. They are kind and funny.

I have one sister. Her name is Zeynep. She is eight years old. I have one brother. His name is Kemal. He is twelve years old.

My grandmother lives with us. Her name is Ayşe. My grandfather lives near our house. His name is Hasan.

I love my family! ❤️
