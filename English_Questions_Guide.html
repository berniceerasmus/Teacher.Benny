Section 1

AM / IS / ARE Questions

Use AM / IS / ARE when there is no main action verb in the sentence.

Cümlede eylem fiili (action verb) YOK ise AM / IS / ARE kullan.

When do we use them? / Ne zaman kullanılır?

✔ Use AM / IS / ARE for

Age, feelings, jobs, nationality, location, and describing things with adjectives.

Yaş, duygular, meslekler, milliyet, konum ve sıfatlarla tanımlama.

✘ Do NOT use for actions

If someone is doing something (eating, working, playing…), use DO / DOES instead.

Birisi bir şey yapıyorsa (yemek yiyor, çalışıyor…) DO / DOES kullan.

Structure / Yapı

AM / IS / ARE + Subject + rest of sentence?

Examples / Örnekler

Question Topic / Konu Turkish Translation
Are you tired? Feeling / Duygu Yorgun musun?
Is she Turkish? Nationality / Milliyet O Türk mü?
Are they at school? Location / Konum Onlar okulda mı?
Am I late? Condition / Durum Geç mi kaldım?
Is he a doctor? Job / Meslek O doktor mu?
Is the coffee hot? Adjective / Sıfat Kahve sıcak mı?
💡 Quick tip: If you can replace the verb with is / are and the sentence still makes sense — use AM / IS / ARE for your question.
Kural: Fiil yerine is / are koyduğunda cümle anlam ifade ediyorsa AM / IS / ARE kullan.
Section 2

DO / DOES Questions

Use DO / DOES when the sentence has a main action verb (play, eat, like, work…).

Cümlede eylem fiili (play, eat, like, work…) VARSA DO / DOES kullan.

Choosing DO vs DOES / DO mu DOES mu?

DO → I, You, We, They

Do you like pizza?
Do they study English?
Do we start at 9?

I, You, We, They ile DO kullan.

DOES → He, She, It

Does she work here?
Does he play football?
Does it hurt?

He, She, It ile DOES kullan.

Structure / Yapı

DO / DOES + Subject + verb (base form)?
⚠️ Important: After DOES, the action verb goes back to its base form — no -s or -es.
DOES kullandıktan sonra fiil eski haline döner — -s / -es eklenmez.

Correct vs Wrong / Doğru ve Yanlış

✔ Correct
Does he play football?

play = base form ✓

✘ Wrong
Does he plays football?

DOES already carries the -s, don't add it again!

✔ Correct
Does she work here?
✘ Wrong
Does she works here?

More examples / Daha fazla örnek

Question Turkish Translation
Do you like coffee? Kahveyi sever misin?
Does he eat meat? O et yer mi?
Do they live near here? Onlar yakında mı oturuyor?
Does your sister speak English? Kız kardeşin İngilizce konuşur mu?
Do you work on weekends? Hafta sonları çalışır mısın?
Section 3

WH- Questions

WH- words come first. They ask for specific information, not just Yes or No.

WH- kelimeleri cümlenin başına gelir. Evet/Hayır yerine bilgi sorar.

WH- Words / Soru Kelimeleri

What
thing / şey
Ne?
Where
place / yer
Nerede? / Nereye?
When
time / zaman
Ne zaman?
Who
person / kişi
Kim?
Why
reason / neden
Neden? / Niçin?
How
method / feeling
Nasıl?

Structure: WH- + BE / WH- + DO/DOES

WH- + AM / IS / ARE + Subject + ...?
WH- + DO / DOES + Subject + verb (base)?

WH- + AM / IS / ARE (no action verb) / Eylem fiili olmadan

Question Turkish Translation
Where are you? Neredesin?
Why is she sad? Neden üzgün?
What is your name? Adın ne?
Who is your teacher? Öğretmenin kim?
When is the meeting? Toplantı ne zaman?
How are you? Nasılsın?

WH- + DO / DOES (with action verb) / Eylem fiili ile

Question Turkish Translation
Where do you live? Nerede yaşıyorsun?
Why do they study English? Neden İngilizce çalışıyorlar?
What does he eat? O ne yiyor?
Who does she work with? Kiminle çalışıyor?
How do you go to work? İşe nasıl gidiyorsun?
When does the class start? Ders ne zaman başlıyor?
💡 Remember: The WH- word asks what type of information you want. Then the rule for the rest of the question is the same as before — AM/IS/ARE for no action verb, DO/DOES for action verbs.
WH- kelimesi ne tür bilgi istediğini söyler. Gerisinde aynı kural geçerli: eylem fiili yoksa AM/IS/ARE, varsa DO/DOES.

📋 Quick Reference / Hızlı Özet

AM / IS / ARE

No action verb. Age, feelings, jobs, places, adjectives.

Eylem fiili yok. Yaş, duygu, meslek, yer, sıfat.

DO

Action verb + I / You / We / They.

I / You / We / They ile eylem fiili.

DOES

Action verb + He / She / It. Verb stays base form!

He / She / It ile eylem fiili. Fiil -s almaz!

WH- First

What, Where, When, Who, Why, How → then normal question order.

WH- kelimesi başa gelir, kalan düzen aynı.
